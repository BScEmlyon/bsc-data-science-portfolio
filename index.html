BSc Data Science for Responsible Business

Student project portfolio

The BSc Data Science for Responsible Business: A joint four-year program by emlyon business school and Centrale Lyon, training AI and Data Science experts with a dual technical and strategic skill set.

The curriculum bridges applied computer science (Development, AI, Data) with field realities, preparing students to design and deploy complex software architectures while tracking profitability. By combining these cutting-edge tech skills with legal, ethical, and algorithmic auditing matters, the program provides the concrete expertise needed to deliver reliable, secure, and useful IT solutions for businesses.

Why this portfolio? Because the best way to understand what our students can do is to look at what they've actually built. This page brings together a selection of real projects completed during the program, offering a concrete look at the technical and strategic skills developed throughout the curriculum.
