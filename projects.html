Hi!ckathon 2025 - AI & Data Science challenge by Hi! PARIS

ML pipeline to predict academic performance from large-scale educational data.

This project designs a machine learning pipeline to predict the math level of 15-year-old students from large-scale educational data. The team trained and tuned three gradient boosting models (XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost) with Optuna, then combined them through Ridge stacking for a more stable final prediction, achieving the 3rd best score among all submissions in the challenge.
